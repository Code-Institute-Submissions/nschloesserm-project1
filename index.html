Manifesto:
Sandwiches of Dublin

Here at Sandwiches of Dublin, we pride ourselves at finding the best sandwich options in the Dublin area. We are here to create an easy to understand guide to help you find those amazing sandwiches nearest to you. In the endevor to do this we hope to find a community of like minded sandwich connoisseurs! We need your help! Have you found the best chicken club, tuna melt, or blt? Please tell us all about it through our contact page! While you are there, sign up for our newsletter to hear about the latest sandwich hotspots in Dublin!
